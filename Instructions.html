Instructions

Robot Revolution

Action
"w/a/s/d" shoot in selected direction
"up" jump
"left" move left
"right" move right

Puzzle
"w" flip vertically
"s" flip horizontally
"a" rotate left
"d" rotate right
"up/left/down/right" select tile
